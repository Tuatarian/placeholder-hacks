24 Hours to code

You and your team of up to 3 people will be given 24 hours to create the best program possible that fits the hackathon's given theme! The majority of the project should be completed during the hackathon hours, but we do allow use of any libraries as well as limited use of code you had written prior to the start of the event.

Practice working with a team!

In the real world, on larger projects, it is exceedingly rare for people to create large projects induvidually. It is much, much more common to find people working in teams to get their projects done. As such, teamwork is the most valuable skill you need to be an effective developer, and this hackathon is designed from the ground up to allow you to practice that. While solo submission is allowed, we recommend you work in a team, and nothing extra will be provided to those who work alone.
